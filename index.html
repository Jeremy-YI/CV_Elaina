About me

Welcome to my world! !

I am a mix media fashion and textiles designer from China, and currently living in Sydney.

I am sharing my design aesthetic through this site, including my uni design work and some other art-related projects.

I think fashion is a medium for conveying stories. I like the deep meaning behind each fashion collection, and present a story through creative photography and props.

I hope that my designs can lead people to think deeply about the meaning behind it, beyond the beauty of the design itself.
